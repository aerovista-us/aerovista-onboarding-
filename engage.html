How to Engage

Purposeful engagement, early

Depending on role and interest, engagement may include:

Review & feedback

  • Strategic document review
  • Domain insight and critique
  • Risk and opportunity spotting

Support & participation

  • Sponsoring a division/product
  • Participating in pilots
  • Helping validate releases
Engagement works best when it’s specific: pick a division, a product, or a release window.
