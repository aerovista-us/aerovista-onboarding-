How Work Is Organized

Divisions with clear ownership

AeroVista is organized into divisions that own outputs and collaborate as needed. Divisions can bill internally and operate semi-independently.

Why divisions

  • Clear accountability
  • Focused delivery
  • Parallel work without chaos

Internal collaboration

  • Divisions can request work from each other
  • Internal billing records value and costs
  • Outputs remain versioned and documented
