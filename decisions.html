How Decisions Are Made

Simple rules to avoid complexity

Documented > remembered

Decisions are captured once, in a reliable place.

Versioned > assumed

Data shapes and systems evolve without breaking old work.

Shipped > debated

Progress is measured in releases and outcomes.

  • Tasks
  • Releases
  • Decisions
